CMS Website Design & Development

Our experienced programming team can provide a sophisticated content management system that provides you with full control over your website content. The user-friendly package allows regular updates to be made to your site, which keeps things fresh and new, without the expense of continuous re-designs.

Our CMS modules are simple and easy to use, no technical knowledge required. You can manage and update your website yourself without anyones help. This allows you to rapidly update information as soon as it's available with you.

Content management system helps to organize the text, images and files for your website within a database. Changes to your website shall be made through the backend. Each page of your website is link to the CMS to retrieve the relevant content for that page, so any update that is made to the content management website is immediately visible online.

Years of experience have shown us that every organization has individual requirements for an effective content management system. Understanding client processes and taking into consideration their immediate and future needs, Golden Resource helps to provide the "right-scale" solution that fits their business case and budget.

Advantages of content managed website

  • CMS Website reduces the maintenance cost
  • CMS eliminates the dependency on web development company
  • CMS helps to maintain current information on the website
  • CMS helps to add as many products you want to display on the website
  • CMS enables you to remove any products which is out dated
  • CMS enables to post any job vacancy in your organization
  • Update news and events on regular basis by any employee
  • CMS maintains the existing and prospective customer's data base
