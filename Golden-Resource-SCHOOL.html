It is a simple yet powerful one-point integrated platform that connects all the departments of an institution namely office, fee counter, academics, activity centre and so on. This online application helps the schools to structuralize the activities and maintain data transparency where needed and properly store data for regular operations and future analysis.

It is a browser based application and thus it is highly acceptable among the users and very easy to manage. Be it desktop, laptops or mobiles! browsing and accessing the contents is easy with the responsive web design layout. The customer does not require any software to be installed in the computer. It has a user-friendly interface. Users with little computer knowledge can use this online application very easily. Ciber SCHOOL comprises different modules which covers each and every department of school and makes the functioning of any educational institute effortless.
