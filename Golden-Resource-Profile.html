About Golden-Resource

"Golden-Resource, driven by professionals with 20 years’ experience in IT Services, Website Designing & development industry." We are a multi-service studio making a difference through effective design, strategy and project management. We love what we do, practice sustainably and make the most of each working day. At Golden-Resource, we believe design and technology has the power to extend our client's overall branding and marketing efforts. We understand our client’s specific requirements, targeted visitors and we design the websites accordingly.

IT Service & Solution | Website Design

Domain Reg. & Hosting | Profile Designing

Google SEO | Social Media Marketing

We are very well experienced in online application development (CRM Applications & CMS Websites), E-Commerce websites with payment gateway integration, specialized and customized website designing, hosting, domain registration, profile designing (company profile in PDF format), logo designing, SEO services, digital marketing, social media marketing, video and CD presentation etc.

Golden Resource is set up with the clear aim to provide quality solutions at an affordable price in the Middle East & Asia. We produce high quality website at a very fast rate. We deliver a consistently high level of customer service to all our clients. Our clients can reach us on Whatsapp / Skype any time. We believe in keeping long term relationship with our valued customers, which will in return provide our customers to be ahead from their competitors.

Our website designers conceptualize and execute designs that will empower your company's identity and business objectives on the internet. Each website that we design is guaranteed to have a professional look and feel that conveys your company's identity in a clear and meaningful way.

Golden Resource, a leading responsive web design company based in UAE & Pakistan, we offer responsive web design services implemented with a series of state-of-the-art techniques to optimize the viewing experience of users across different screen sizes and resolutions of various devices ranging from smart phones, tablet PCs, desktops and various sizes of laptop screens.

We would welcome your esteemed organization as our valued client and would like to assure you the complete web based business solution from the scratch to execution.
